Flip Dash is a simple but addictive 2D arcade game.

Tap/Click/Press Space key to flip your player between the top and bottom paths.

Avoid obstacles that come your way.

Collect points and challenge yourself to beat your high score.

Test your reaction speed and timing in this endless runner style challenge.

Also you can Change your skin.

🕹️ Controls

Keyboard / Mobile Input: Press [Space] or tap screen to flip.
