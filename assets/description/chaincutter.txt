Chain Cutter is a fun and challenging physics-based puzzle game where strategy, timing, and precision are the keys to success. Your objective is simple: cut chains in the correct order to guide objects safely to their destination. While the concept is easy to learn, each level introduces new obstacles, mechanics, and challenges that will test your problem-solving skills.

Analyze the environment, predict object movements, and carefully decide which chains to cut. A single mistake can send objects in the wrong direction, while the perfect sequence can lead to a satisfying solution. As you progress, levels become more complex, requiring creativity, planning, and quick thinking.

Featuring smooth physics, engaging puzzles, and rewarding gameplay, Chain Cutter provides an enjoyable experience for casual players and puzzle enthusiasts alike. Every level offers a unique challenge that encourages experimentation and strategic decision-making.
